Our Mission

When a college student is faced with a confusing point, they confront a jungle of resources to cut through. On the other side, the instructors spend hours clarifying the same concepts over and over again. We found that the most pressing problem, therefore, is the lack of feedback between instructors and students on what is working for the studentsâ€™ understanding and what is not.



Kindergarten provides instructors with quick and easy methods for gathering student feedback and streamlining office hours.
